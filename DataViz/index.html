The First Comparison

This graph represents the percentage of Navient cases classified as stundent loan cases during March 13, 2020, and March 13, 2022. These dates represent the period of the pandemic (though we might still be in it, but it began in March 2020). The red represents Colorado's number of student loan cases during that time period, 44 cases. Meanwhile, the blue represents Massachusetts' student loan cases during the same period, 91 cases. Pie Chart
Infogram

The are two sub-products for student loan cases, federal student loan servicing and private student loan. For Massachusetts, 55 cases were related to federal loans, while 36 cases were related to private loans. As for Colorado, 25 cases were related to federal loans and 19 were related to private loans.
