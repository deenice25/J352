The Final Fantasy Franchise Timeline

This is a timeline that includes all of the Final Fantasy franchise mainline games. It is intended to help anyone, gamer or not, understand how many Final Fantasy games there are and how they are not connected unless the mainline game developed into a trilogy or series. It is to give the reader a gist of how the franchise is and how many games there are. Also, if the reader decides to watch the a video or all of them, they might want to play the game. Overall, the timeline is to help the reader understand the mainline games of one of the most popular gaming franchise. This could help them get into gaming or try out new games.
